# ErpProject

An integrated ERP system designed for efficient enterprise resource management and business process automation. It streamlines core HR and administrative operations, including payroll, bidding, attendance, and leave management.

---

## 📋 Project Overview

* **Project Name**: ErpProject
* **Duration**: 2026.08 ~ 2026.10

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Payroll Calculation**: Automated calculation of salaries, deductions, and tax withholding for employees.
* **Bidding Service**: Comprehensive management of corporate bidding processes and real-time data handling.
* **Attendance Management**: Tracking employee clock-in/out times and calculating total working hours.
* **Leave Management**: Management of annual leave accrual and the leave request/approval workflow.
